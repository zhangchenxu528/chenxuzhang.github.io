Biography

Chenxu Zhang is a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate with the department of computer science, the University of Texas at Dallas. His academic advisor is Prof. Xiaohu Guo. He received his B.S. degree in software engineering in 2015 and M.S. degree in computer science in 2018, both from Beihang University, Beijing.

His research interests include Computer Graphics, Computer Vision, and AI, with a focus on Talking Face Generation, Conversational Gestures Synthesis, Deblur-NeRF with Human Motion, and Emotional Talking Avatar.
